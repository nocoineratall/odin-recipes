Ciambellone

Description

With the following recipe you are going to bake a delicious Ciambellone. Your Nonna will be proud!

Ingredients

  • Flour - 500g
  • Sugar - 250g
  • Yogurt - 125g
  • Milk - 125g
  • Oil - 125g
  • Eggs - 4 pieces
  • Yeast - 10g

Steps

  1. Put yogurt in a bowl
  2. Drop the powders and mix properly
  3. Add the eggs - keep mixing
  4. Pour slowly the milk and then the oil
  5. Mix for at least 5 mins
  6. Pour the dough in a suitable die
  7. Bake @ 180Â°C for 35mins - adjust time accordingly

Pro tip: use a toothpick to check for the perfect softness
