Cous-Cous

Description

With the following recipe you are going to bake a delicious Cous-cous-vegetariano. Invite your friend for a spicy launch!

Ingredients

  • Vegetables
  • Chicken
  • Cous-cous

Steps

  1. Bring the cooking liquid to a boil in a medium pot
  2. Add the couscous
  3. Use a fork to fluff it up and break up the clumps for light and fluffy couscous
  4. Add the chicken and vegetables

Pro tip: The couscous can be frozen for up to 3 months. When ready to serve, reheat it in the microwave until hot.
